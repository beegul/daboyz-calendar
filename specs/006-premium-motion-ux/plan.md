## Summary

Upgrade daboyz-calender into a world-class, premium digital experience with fluid motion, micro-interactions, modern aesthetics, and flawless responsiveness. Inspired by Linear, Apple, and Stripe.

**Technical Approach (Cutting-Edge)**:
- **Framer Motion** (~35 kB): Orchestrate complex staggered animations, FLIP layout morphing, gesture-driven transitions
- **react-use-gesture** (~6 kB): Velocity-based swipe gestures for mobile month navigation
- **Custom React Hooks** (~5 kB): useOptimisticUpdate, useGestureSwipe, usePrefersReducedMotion
- **Tailwind Animation Extensions** (~1 kB): Design tokens for easing, duration, motion utilities
- **Accessibility-First** (Zero cost): WCAG 2.1 AA/AAA, prefers-reduced-motion support, 44×44px touch targets

**Total Bundle Impact**: ~47 kB gzipped (within 30-50 kB budget).
